THIS IS AN EXAMPLE SHOW FOLDER
==============================

Rename this folder to your show's name (e.g. "Family Adventures"),
then drop the episode files in, named like:

    S01E01 - The Beginning.mp4
    S01E02 - The Road Trip.mp4
    S01E03 - Are We There Yet.mp4

...or just delete this whole folder if you don't need it.

The folder name becomes the show title. Episodes are sorted by
their season/episode number, and "Up Next" auto-plays the next one.
